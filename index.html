Reservations

We require your credit card details to secure your reservation. Your information is held by a secure third party and no charges are made before the event.

You will receive an SMS reminder of your booking 48 hours before you dine with us.

Cancellation Policy

We are a small restaurant where table space is limited. We love to share our food with a full and vibrant room. Cancellations and no-shows often result in other diners missing out.

If you need to modify or cancel your reservation please contact us at least 24 hours prior to your booking. If you notify us within 24 hours and we cannot fill your spot you will incur a $65 per person fee on your credit card.

Thanks for your assistance and understanding.

Group Bookings

We welcome bookings for up to 6 persons.

Groups of 6 partake in our special Chef's Menu for $65 per person.
